About Us

This site is created by 2 NUS students as part of their Orbital project summer 2014. The aim is to help users easily record down the memorable moments associated with the attached photos so they can recall these moments when they look at the photo in the future. Users can either share these moments with their friends on Facebook or keep them as collection by downloading the completed work as a PDF file.
